WHAT THIS APP DOES

READ

Choose a comic or book from the Library. It opens in page form with a cinematic frame.

LISTEN

Each book has its own soundtrack. YouTube playlists sit under the pages while you read.

HEAR IT READ ALOUD

Visit the Audio section to hear my books read aloud, page by page.

HOW TO USE THIS APP

  • Go to Library to pick a comic or book.
  • Click a title to open it in the Reader.
  • Pages turn with a comic or book frame depending on the type.
  • The matching YouTube playlist plays under the pages.
  • Go to Audio to hear read‑aloud versions.
  • Use the Hero / Villain button to switch light / dark comic themes.
